1. Information We Collect

PulseLineDaily collects the following information only when voluntarily provided by users:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and phone number, provided when subscribing to newsletters or contacting us.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device information, and browsing behavior collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve news content, website features, and services.
  • Send newsletters, updates, and promotional communications (only if you opt-in).
  • Respond to inquiries, feedback, or support requests.
  • Analyze website traffic to improve user experience.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal basis for processing your personal information includes:

  • Consent: When you voluntarily subscribe to newsletters or provide your information.
  • Legitimate Interests: For website analytics, improving content, and maintaining security.
  • Legal Obligations: Where required by applicable law.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

PulseLineDaily does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our website, sending newsletters, or performing analytics.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • In connection with a business transfer, merger, or acquisition.

5. Cookies and Tracking

PulseLineDaily uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and deliver relevant content. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

6. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Withdraw consent for certain data processing activities.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce agreements.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

9. Third-Party Services

PulseLineDaily may use third-party services such as analytics tools or email distribution providers. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Users are encouraged to review their privacy policies directly.

10. Children’s Privacy

PulseLineDaily is not directed to children under 13 years of age (or the applicable local age). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date. We encourage users to review this policy regularly.

12. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us.
